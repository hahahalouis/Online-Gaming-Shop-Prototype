Counter Strike:Global Offensive


Price: $99.00



ABOUT THIS GAME

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a round-based, 5v5 tactical FPS with an Attackers vs. Defenders setup and no respawns.


meaning if a player is eliminated they will not respawn until the next round. The game is available to download from the STEAM Games Client.

KEY FEATURES

  • This is the 4th entry in the Counter-Strike series and has consistently stood the test of time as a Tier 1 esport along with the likes of League of Legends and DOTA 2.
  • The “Bomb Defusal” mode is the standard and accepted game mode for CS:GO competition.
  • Attackers attempt to plant a bomb at one of two sites. Defenders will try to stop them before planting or defuse the bomb if planted.
  • The first team to win 16 rounds will win the game.
  • In-between rounds, players can purchase weapons, items/utility, and armor using in-game “cash”. The amount of earned cash depends on previous round performance.
